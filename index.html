Math For Fun


Originally created with my son in mind, this game lets you have fun with math! First choose how many players (one/two). Then choose your difficulty (easy, medium, or hard). Finally, choose your category (addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division). Your score will be calculated based on the difficulty you select, and how large the numbers you're presented are.
Itching to test your math skills? You can clone the project here.
